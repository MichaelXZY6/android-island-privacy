Android Island — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-05

Android Island ("the app") is a floating overlay that mimics Apple's Dynamic Island. This policy explains exactly what data the app touches and what it does not do.

Data we collect

None. The app has no servers, no analytics, no tracking SDKs, no advertising libraries. Nothing leaves your device.

Permissions we request, and why

  • RECORD_AUDIO — Used solely to obtain real-time FFT (frequency spectrum) data via Android's android.media.audiofx.Visualizer API while music is playing on your device. The audio buffer is read for ~10 ms windows, the spectrum is computed locally on-device, and then the buffer is immediately discarded. Audio is never recorded, saved, uploaded, or transmitted anywhere. No microphone audio is captured — only the system's own music output mix is read for visualization.
  • READ_PHONE_STATE / ANSWER_PHONE_CALLS — To display incoming-call information on the island and provide accept/decline buttons. Phone numbers are shown briefly on screen and never logged or transmitted.
  • READ_CONTACTS / READ_CALL_LOG — To resolve caller names from your local contact list. All lookups are on-device only.
  • BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE — To read incoming notifications so they can be displayed on the island. Notification text is rendered to screen; nothing is stored or transmitted.
  • BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE — Required by Android to host a TYPE_ACCESSIBILITY_OVERLAY window (the floating island) that remains visible on the lock screen and over system Settings. Also used to suppress the system's heads-up notification animation when the user has opted in. The service inspects only window-state events from com.android.systemui to determine when a heads-up window appears; no other app's content is read.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Used for the foreground status notification only. No content is generated or sent.
  • VIBRATE — Haptic feedback on island taps.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — Asks the user (via the system dialog) to whitelist the app from battery-freezing background optimisations, otherwise the AccessibilityService is killed and the island stops working.

Data sharing with third parties

None. The app has no third-party SDKs.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If the data practices ever change, this page will be updated. There is no built-in update notification because the app collects nothing, so users have no account to notify.
